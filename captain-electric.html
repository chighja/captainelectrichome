About the band

Captain Electric is a five-piece out of Frederick, Maryland, built for bars, breweries, festivals, and the occasional wedding that wanted something with a little more volume.

We pull from two decades of pop, alt-rock, and emo — the kind of set where the whole room ends up shouting the bridge back at us. No backing tracks, no laptop. Just the five of us, plugged in.

Available for ticketed shows and private events across Maryland, DC, and Virginia.

The big singalongs

The crowd-favorite anthems that fill a dance floor in one chord.

The emo nostalgia run

2000s Warped Tour energy for everyone who owned the eyeliner.

The current stuff

Newer pop and alt hits so the set never feels like a time capsule.
